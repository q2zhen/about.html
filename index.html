Projects

When We Come Together

This year, we were fortunate to be selected to participate in the Light on Stratford light show and create the project "When We Come Together." Designed around the theme of reconciliation, the work is an interactive microwall. Viewers' actions and touches trigger changes in the lighting, symbolizing the connection and understanding between different individuals. Through this piece, we hope that viewers will not only appreciate the beauty of light but also reflect on the reconciliation and integration between people and cultures.

Project 2

Earlier this year, my team and I created an interactive game about accessibility and health, designed to help players identify and understand food allergies. Through an interactive experience, the game allows users to learn about food allergies in a safe and fun environment, raising awareness of accessibility and healthy eating.
